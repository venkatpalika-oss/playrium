# Playrium Originals Roadmap Prioritization

This document tracks the relative priority of upcoming Tier A Originals. It should be revisited after each Original completes Phase 5 Human Testing, since validated learnings, for example about what drives replayability or which interaction patterns confuse testers, may shift priorities for the experiences that follow.

> Note: The scores below are prioritization hypotheses based on design-phase reasoning, not validated data. They are expected to be replaced by findings from each Original's Phase 1 Experience Evaluation and Phase 5 Human Testing as that evidence becomes available.

## Current Status
Sand Art Studio (TM) has completed Phases 1 through 4 and is currently frozen at Phase 5, Human Testing, awaiting real user data. No other Original should begin in-depth design work until that data is available, since it is expected to meaningfully inform the experiences below.

## Scoring Framework
Each concept is scored across five dimensions plus an overall priority rank: Originality, Emotional Impact, Replayability, Technical Risk (lower risk scores higher), and Build Time (shorter time scores higher). Scores below reflect current thinking based on the design phases completed so far and are expected to be refined once Sand Art Studio Phase 5 data is available.

## Phase A: Creative Flagships
1. Sand Art Studio (TM), in progress, frozen at Phase 5.
2. Kaleidoscope Studio (TM), high originality and strong visual differentiation, likely benefits from Sand Art Studio's findings on replayability and anticipation.
3. Magnet Lab (TM), strong physical-interaction novelty, moderate technical risk around physics feel.

## Phase B: Relaxation Collection
4. Wind Chimes (TM), strong ambient, low-pressure appeal, likely lower technical risk and shorter build time.
5. Color Harmony (TM), solid but currently the lowest-differentiation concept in the roadmap, worth revisiting once more Originals establish the relaxation collection's identity.

## Phase C: Spectacle Collection
6. Fireworks Festival (TM), high emotional impact potential, particularly around a climactic Magic Moment, moderate build complexity.
7. Weather Creator (TM), high impact and strong differentiation, but likely the most technically ambitious concept on this list.
8. Ice Sculpture (TM), solid concept, currently lower priority pending validated learnings from the Creative Flagships tier.

## Next Action
No new Original moves into Phase 2, Experience Design, until Sand Art Studio (TM) completes Phase 5 and, if triggered, Phase 5.5. Once that data is in hand, re-score this table using the Experience Evaluation Template for whichever concept is prioritized next, incorporating specific findings. For example, if replayability is found to come from anticipation rather than complexity, weight that criterion accordingly for Kaleidoscope Studio and Magnet Lab specifically, since they are next in sequence.
