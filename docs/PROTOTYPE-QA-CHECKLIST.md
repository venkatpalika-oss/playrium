# Prototype QA Checklist (Phase 4)

Complete this checklist internally before any prototype is presented for Phase 5 Human Testing. This is an internal quality gate, not a substitute for human testing.

## Interaction Quality
- Is the core interaction discoverable without any onscreen instructions?
- Does the interaction respond immediately and smoothly to input, with no perceptible lag?
- Is there exactly one clear Magic Moment, not several competing high points?

## Engagement Quality
- Does the design plausibly encourage replay, not just a single pass-through?
- Is there a natural stopping point, or does the experience just trail off without a sense of completion?

## Interface Quality
- Does the experience avoid unnecessary UI chrome during the core interaction?
- Are any visible controls self-explanatory without a text label?

## Technical Quality
- Does the prototype perform smoothly on a mid-range mobile device, not only on a development machine?
- Are there any console errors or visual glitches during a full run-through?
- Does the experience work correctly with sound muted?

## Sign-Off
This checklist should be explicitly signed off, item by item, before scheduling Phase 5 sessions. Any unresolved "no" answers should be addressed or consciously accepted as a known risk before testers are recruited.
