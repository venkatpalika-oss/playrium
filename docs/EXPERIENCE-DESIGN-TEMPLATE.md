# Experience Design Template (Phase 2)

Complete this once an Experience Evaluation has reached a Go decision.

## Creative Vision
A short descriptive paragraph capturing the feeling the experience should evoke, written the way you would describe it to someone who will never see the code.

## Core Interaction Loop
Describe the smallest repeatable unit of interaction: what the user does, what changes as a result, and what invites them to do it again.

## Magic Moment
Name the single Magic Moment this experience will center on, and describe exactly what happens, visually and emotionally, at that instant.

## Audio Direction
Describe the ambient soundscape, any interaction-triggered sounds, and specifically what happens, sonically, at the Magic Moment.

## Visual Direction
Describe the palette, lighting approach, and overall visual mood, consistent with the Visual Language section of the Design Bible.

## UX Flow
Outline the path from arrival to the Magic Moment to any post-experience actions such as sharing, replaying, or exiting.

## Interaction Rules
List the specific rules governing how input maps to response: what triggers what, and any constraints, such as cooldowns, limits, or sequencing, that shape the interaction.

## Success Criteria
State what "working as designed" looks like before this moves to prototyping, phrased as observable outcomes rather than technical specifications.
