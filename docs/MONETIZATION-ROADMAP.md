# Monetization Roadmap

Documents the phased monetization approach decided in issue #17, so the plan and its reasoning are preserved outside of a single issue thread.

## Philosophy

Rather than jumping straight to ads, monetize in phases that fit Playrium's brand as a premium, culture-forward experience library. Each phase should only be pursued once the previous phase (and the underlying experience library and traffic) has matured enough to support it.

## Phases

1. **Free tier** - The current state. All experiences are free to play, no account required. This remains the foundation; nothing above should degrade this core experience.
2. **Premium Experiences** - A subset of new, higher-production-value experiences offered behind a one-time purchase or subscription, while the existing free library stays free.
3. **Teacher Collections** - Curated bundles of experiences framed around classroom use (e.g. a country's culture, math/physics concepts), packaged for educators.
4. **Museum Collections** - Similar curation, but packaged for museums/cultural institutions to license or embed (e.g. an interactive kiosk tie-in).
5. **Tourism Partnerships** - Collaborations with tourism boards or cultural organizations to co-brand or sponsor country-specific experience sets.
6. **Brand Collaborations** - Sponsored or co-branded experiences with aligned consumer brands, once Playrium has enough traffic/credibility to make this attractive without feeling exploitative.

## Notes

- This order is intentional: each phase builds credibility and audience for the next, and phases are ordered from least to most dependent on scale/reputation.
- Revisit this prioritization once the experience library and traffic grow - this is a starting plan, not a locked-in commitment.
- This document is descriptive of a product/business plan already decided by the repo owner in issue #17; it is not financial or investment advice.
