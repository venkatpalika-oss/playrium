KOINOBORI FESTIVAL

Koinobori Festival

"Ride the wind. Race the sky."

Steer your koinobori left and right to stay inside the rising wind columns. Drift out of the wind and you'll sink. Weave through the bamboo poles as you climb. Reach the top of the sky to complete the festival.
